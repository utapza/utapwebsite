## When Delivery Makes Sense

We're honest about this:

✅ **Use Mr D / Uber Eats when:**
- You're off campus and ordering from restaurants that aren't in your area
- It's late at night and no campus vendor is open
- You genuinely cannot leave where you are

❌ **Don't use delivery apps when:**
- You have 15 minutes between lectures (driver won't arrive in time anyway)
- The café is a 3-minute walk from your res
- You're ordering from a campus vendor that's already on uTap

## The 3 uTap Rules for Campus Ordering

**Rule 1: Order before you leave.**
Place your uShop order 10–15 minutes before you're done in class. Walk up, tap your NFC card, collect your order. Zero queue time.

**Rule 2: Pay exactly what's on the menu.**
uTap charges you zero markups, zero delivery fees, zero service fees. The price on the app is the price you pay.

**Rule 3: Use the free tier. Upgrade only if you want extras.**
The core uTap experience—NFC campus access, uShop ordering, event discovery—is completely free. Premium upgrades (extra card slots, multi-device login, uGig events access, verified badge) are there for power users who want more.
