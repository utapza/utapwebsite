Today, uTap goes live at Nelson Mandela University.

If you're an NMU student, here's what that means for you:

- **Your student card lives on your phone.** Scan it once with the uTap app. From that moment, you tap your phone at campus access points exactly as you would your physical card.
- **Order campus food without delivery fees.** Browse NMU vendors on uShop, pay the menu price — nothing added — and collect with a pickup code.
- **Access exclusive student events via uGig** (Premium feature).

The app is free. The card wallet is free. The food ordering is free. Download it in 2 minutes and scan your card.

## Why we started with NMU

NMU was the right first campus for a few reasons.

The student card infrastructure is compatible with our NFC scanning system. The campus has a dense mix of food vendors, residences, and academic buildings that make pickup-first ordering genuinely useful. And the team has existing relationships on the ground that helped us understand what campus life at NMU actually looks like — not a generic version of it.

We're a small team. We didn't want to launch at 10 campuses and do it poorly. We wanted to launch at one campus, do it well, learn from real students and real vendors, and earn the right to expand.

NMU is that campus.

---

## What's live today

**For students:**

- NFC card scanning — scan your physical NMU student card into the app. Your phone becomes your campus card.
- uShop — browse and order from NMU campus vendors. Pay the menu price. Collect with a pickup code.
- Cloud backup — if you get a new phone, your card is restored from your account. No rescanning.
- Core app is completely free.

**For vendors:**

- Free to list on uShop (one store)
- Zero transaction commission
- Pickup code system — orders come in clearly, students collect cleanly
- Yoco-powered checkout — same payment infrastructure many vendors already use

If you're a vendor at NMU and you're not on uShop yet, [sign up at vendors.utaptech.co.za](https://vendors.utaptech.co.za). Setup takes 15 minutes.

## What we're not saying

We're not claiming uTap is finished. It's not.

Card compatibility across all NMU card types is still expanding. There are edge cases in the NFC scan flow we're still smoothing out. The vendor analytics dashboard is minimal. uGig's full event discovery features are still in development.

We're launching because the core experience — card on phone, order without fees, collect with a code — works. And the only way to learn the rest is from real students using it for real things.

If something doesn't work for you: [report it at utaptech.co.za/help](/help) or message us directly. We respond.
