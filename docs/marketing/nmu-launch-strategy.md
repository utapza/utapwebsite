# uTap NMU Alpha Launch Strategy (Pivot)

*Last Updated: 2026-05-15*
*Status: Alpha Phase (PE Pilot)*
*Features: uShop (Ordering) + uGig (Events)*
*Constraint: No NFC / No Card Scanning*

---

## 1. The Goal
To prove operational reliability for campus ordering and event discovery in a controlled, 100-student environment. We are testing **trust** and **retention** above all else.

- **Primary Metric:** Vendor retention after 4 weeks of remote support.
- **Secondary Metric:** Order frequency per student.

---

## 2. The Core Message
**Skip the queue. Never miss a gig.**

- **For Students:** "Order your lunch before class ends, pick it up without the queue. Discover every campus event in one place."
- **For Vendors:** "Keep 100% of your earnings for the first 6 months. No delivery commissions. No messy WhatsApp orders."

## 3. Mission-Critical Materials

### Physical Pack (Minimum Quantities)
- **Founding Collaborator Certificates (10):** Formal recognition to build vendor trust.
- **QR Code Counter Flyers (25):** 2-3 per vendor; "Skip the queue" hook.
- **Available on uTap Stickers (15):** Window/counter branding.
- **Recruitment A5 Flyers (50):** Hand-to-hand during class talks.
- **Pitch One-Pagers (10):** Physical backup for vendor discovery meetings.

### Digital Assets
- **Alpha Student WhatsApp Group:** Invite link ready for Class Talk slides.
- **Founding Vendor WhatsApp Group:** Ready for personally adding vendors.
- **Facebook Page (uTap NMU):** Active; 3 posts staged for launch week.
- **TestFlight / APK Landing Page:** Mobile-friendly download path.
- **Vendor Support Videos:** 1-minute "How to accept an order" shared on WhatsApp.

---

## 4. Physical Trip: 5-Day Roadmap (Port Elizabeth)

### Day 1: The Foundation of Trust
- **Action:** Face-to-face meetings with 5–10 founding vendors.
- **Hook:** Listen to their current pain points. Do not pitch features yet.
- **Demo:** Show the Yoco money flow. Prove the money goes to their account.
- **Goal:** Get the "Yes" from at least 5 vendors.

### Day 2: Digital Shelf Setup
- **Action:** Hands-on onboarding. Take photos of menus, set hours, list stores.
- **Student Side:** Deliver first Class Talk. Hook: "Skip the queue."
- **Distribution:** Share TestFlight (iOS) and APK (Android) links directly.
- **Goal:** Stores are live; first 25 students registered.

### Day 3: The Moment of Truth
- **Action:** Shadow vendors during the morning rush. Fix tech friction in real-time.
- **Student Side:** Second Class Talk to hit 100-student cap.
- **Recruitment:** Close recruitment once 100 are in. Add them to the Alpha WhatsApp group.
- **Goal:** 100 students onboarded; first 50 orders processed.

### Day 4: Hands-Off Stress Test
- **Action:** Observe from a distance. Let vendors trade independently.
- **Feedback:** Conduct quick 2-minute interviews with students at pickup points.
- **Goal:** Zero critical failures; vendors managing orders without hand-holding.

### Day 5: Handover & Celebration
- **Action:** Present "Founding Collaborator" certificates to vendors.
- **Support:** Set up the Vendor Support WhatsApp group. Walk through the remote support process.
- **Goal:** Exit PE with a functional, self-sustaining loop.

---

## 5. Remote Support Model (Weeks 2–6)
*Support shifts from physical presence to Joburg-based remote.*

- **Daily Check-ins:** 09:00 WhatsApp check-in with all vendors.
- **Weekly Review:** Sunday night metrics summary shared with the team.
- **Metric Tracking:**
  - uShop orders processed.
  - uGig events booked.
  - Payout confirmation (Every Friday).

---

## 6. Metrics for Success

| Metric | Target (Alpha) | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Student Installs | 100 | Manageable support load. |
| Orders Processed | 500+ | Proves utility and habit. |
| Event Bookings | 50+ | Tests uGig discovery loop. |
| Vendor Retention | 100% | Proves the product is worth the effort for stalls. |
| First Payout | Success | The ultimate trust-builder. |

---

## 7. What We Are NOT Doing (Out of Scope)
- **NFC / Card Scanning:** No physical card digitization.
- **Public Launch:** No posters, no billboards, no massive social push.
- **Generic Gigs:** No freelancing or task-based work. uGig is for Events only.
- **Delivery:** No drivers. Pickup only.
