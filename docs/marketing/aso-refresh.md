## 2. Play Store (Android) Listing

### App Name (50 characters)
uTap: Student Card, NFC Wallet & Campus Food

### Short Description (80 characters)
Your student card on your phone. Order campus food with zero delivery fees.

### Long Description
**The only app you need for campus life in South Africa.**

uTap is a digital wallet built by students, for students. We’ve digitised the campus experience so you can ditch the plastic student card and the expensive delivery fees.

**NFC STUDENT CARD WALLET**
Lost your student card? Not anymore. uTap lets you scan your physical NFC student card and store it securely on your phone.
- Tap into campus gates and residences using your phone.
- Save on card replacement fees (R80-R250 at most SA universities).
- Works with MIFARE-standard student cards.

**USHOP: ZERO-FEE CAMPUS ORDERING**
Stop paying "delivery" fees for a 3-minute walk.
- Browse vendors at NMU.
- Order ahead for pickup.
- Pay menu prices — no delivery fees, no service charges.
- Skip the long lunch queues with instant pickup codes.

**UGIG: CAMPUS EVENTS**
Get exclusive access to the best student events, parties, and workshops on campus. One profile for all your campus access.

**SUPPORTED CAMPUSES:**
uTap is currently live at **Nelson Mandela University (NMU)**. We are expanding to more South African universities throughout 2026. Download the app to register your interest for your campus!

**GET STARTED IN 2 MINUTES:**
1. Install uTap.
2. Verify your student email.
3. Scan your card.
4. Leave your wallet at home.

*uTap handles your campus identity securely. We don’t store your data in a readable format on our servers — your campus credentials stay between you and your university's access system.*
