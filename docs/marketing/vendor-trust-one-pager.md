## Where does the money go?

When a student pays on uTap, here's what happens — step by step:

**1. Student pays at checkout**
Payment is processed by **Yoco** — South Africa's most trusted payment provider for small businesses. The same Yoco you may already use at your till.

**2. You see it immediately**
The moment the order is placed, it appears on your **uTap Vendor Dashboard** — the exact amount you are owed, in real time.

**3. It settles into your account**
By default, your earnings settle into your bank account on the **28th of every month**.

**4. Need it sooner?**
Request a payout any time directly from your dashboard. Minimum R50. No waiting.

## What if something goes wrong?

**Pickup code doesn't work:** Give the student their order and message us immediately. We will manually verify and ensure you get paid.

**App is slow or you can't see an order:** Call the founder line. We will fix it on the spot or credit the order manually.

**After we leave PE:** We return to Joburg but support continues. Daily check-in messages, same-day responses to issues, and we personally confirm your first payout lands correctly.
