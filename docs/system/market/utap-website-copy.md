- We won't sell student data.

## 7. Pricing (`/pricing`)

The most-read page after the homepage. Be transparent.

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| Headline | `Pricing without the surprise.` |
| Sub | `Free to list. A small commission on each paid order. Every cent shown on your statement.` |
| Section: Listing | `Free` — `List your store and your products. We don't charge to be on uTap.` |
| Section: Commission | `{X}% per paid order` — `Charged to the vendor on each successful order. Reduces your payout, never the student's total.` |
| Section: Yoco fee | `{Y}% + VAT per transaction` — `Paid to Yoco, not to uTap. Shown separately on your statement.` |
| Section: Payouts | `R 50 minimum, on demand or on the 28th` — `Auto-payouts go to your Yoco bank account on the 28th of every month. You can request one any time, with a R 50 minimum.` |
| Example block | A worked example: `R 100 order = R {Yoco} Yoco fee + R {platform} uTap fee = R {net} to you.` |
| Pricing FAQ | At least 5 questions, all about money. |
| CTA | `Open my store` |

Always defer the *actual* percentage values to live config (`finance_settings`). The website should render these from a single source so they don't drift.

## 8. Help (`/help`)

A categorised FAQ. Each category has 5–10 questions; each answer is 1–3 sentences with optional links.

Categories:

- `Getting started`
- `Scanning your student card`
- `Orders and pickup`
- `Payments and refunds`
- `Tickets`
- `Vendors`
- `Account and privacy`

### 8.1 Tone for help articles

- Direct answer in the first sentence.
- "If…" patterns for branching ("If your phone has NFC, scanning works. If not, you can still use uShop and uGig.").
- Link to the relevant in-app spot, not a paragraph elsewhere on the site.

## 13. Forbidden on the marketing site

- "Revolutionising / disrupting / reimagining" anything.
- "AI-powered" or any AI marketing.
- "Trusted by thousands" if it's not numerically true.
- Stock photos of generic students smiling at laptops.
- Anything that implies we're a delivery service.
- Hero videos that autoplay with sound.
